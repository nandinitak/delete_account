🔒 Account Deletion Policy

In accordance with Google Play's User Data policy, SecureHomePlus allows users to request the permanent deletion of their account and all associated data. This action is irreversible.

  • Authentication Data: Your login credentials and profile information will be wiped from our secure servers (Firebase).
  • Local Records: Any evaluation surveys or safety reports stored locally in the app will be cleared upon account deletion from within the app.
  • Processing Time: Requests submitted via web are processed within 7 business days.

Request Deletion

To request account deletion, please enter your registered email address below. A confirmation link will be sent to you.

Submit Deletion Request

Alternatively, you can delete your account directly from within the app by going to My Account > Delete Account.
