Delete Account – SmartEduQuiz

If you wish to delete your SmartEduQuiz account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

🔹 Option 1: Delete from App

  1. Open the SmartEduQuiz app
  2. Go to Profile / Settings
  3. Tap on Delete Account
  4. Confirm your request

🔹 Option 2: Request via Email

Email us at: your-email@gmail.com

Please include your registered email ID.

🗑️ Data Deleted

  • User profile information (name, email)
  • Quiz progress and scores
  • App usage data

⏳ Data Retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for legal and security purposes, after which it is permanently deleted.
